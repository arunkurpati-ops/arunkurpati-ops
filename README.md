## 🎯 About Me

Hello! I'm **Arun Kurpati**, a passionate **Healthcare Data Analyst & Medical Informatics Graduate Student** based in **Fargo, North Dakota**. I specialize in transforming complex clinical data into actionable insights that improve patient outcomes.

- 🏥 **Healthcare Analytics**: Clinical data, claims processing, and patient outcomes
- 🤖 **Machine Learning**: Predictive modeling, interpretability, and ethical AI in healthcare
- 📊 **Data Science**: Feature engineering, statistical analysis, and BI dashboards
- 🔒 **Healthcare Compliance**: HIPAA, GDPR, data privacy, and ethical guidelines
- 🧬 **Bioinformatics**: Next-generation sequencing and genomics data analysis
- 🎓 Currently pursuing **Medical Informatics** at Harrisburg University
- 💼 Ex **AstraZeneca Healthcare** | **Global Health Limited**
- 🌱 Learning advanced **AI/ML applications in clinical decision support**
- ⚡ Fun fact: I believe data is the new stethoscope for modern healthcare!
